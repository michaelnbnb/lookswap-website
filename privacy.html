Your privacy is important to us

Effective date: 20 Oct 2025
Owner/Controller: @LookSwap.ai • Contact: support@lookswap.ai

1) What we collect

Account & identifiers: Firebase UID; optional name/email (e.g., Sign in with Apple); appAccountToken (lowercased UUID); device identifiers provided by Apple/Firebase (e.g., App Check token); IP address and basic device info in server logs.

Transactions: product IDs, transaction IDs, subscription status, receipts, App Store Server Notifications.

Usage & balance: plan, points/credits granted and consumed, request IDs, ratelimit counters, event history.

Images you upload & outputs: photos you submit for tryon and generated images.

Support messages: info you send to support.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, photos outside what you explicitly pick, or sensitive personal data.

2) How we use data

  • Provide the service, generate images, maintain your balance, and prevent abuse.
  • Verify purchases and subscriptions with Apple; process consumables.
  • Secure the service (App Check verification, ratelimiting, fraud prevention).
  • Troubleshoot, analytics, and service performance (aggregate, nonadvertising).
  • Communicate with you about the service.

We do not sell personal data or use thirdparty advertising SDKs.

3) Image processing & retention

  • Uploaded images are used only to generate your results.
  • Generated images are stored in Google Cloud Storage and shared back via timelimited signed URLs.
  • We target 24 hours retention for generated files and keep request/processing records up to 30 days for reliability and abuse investigations. You may delete results sooner by emailing support.

4) Sharing

Processors: Google Firebase/Google Cloud (Auth, Firestore, Storage), Generative AI (image generation), Apple (inapp purchases, App Store Server Notifications).

Required by law & safety: if necessary to comply with legal process, prevent fraud, or protect users.

Business transfers: in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice as required.

5) Security

Industrystandard security, encryption in transit, leastprivilege access, and abuse protections (App Check, rate limits). No method is 100% secure.

6) Your choices & rights

  • Access, update, or delete your account data; request a copy; object to or restrict certain processing where applicable.
  • California & other regions: you have rights to know, delete, and optout of sale/sharing (we don't sell).
  • Request via support@lookswap.ai. We may retain limited records to meet legal/accounting requirements and to enforce platform abuse limits.

7) Children

Not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your region). If we learn we collected such data, we'll delete it.

8) International transfers

Data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our processors operate, with appropriate safeguards.

9) Changes

We'll post updates here and adjust the "Effective date." Material changes will be notified inapp or by email where required.
