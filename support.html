Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI virtual try-on work? +
LookSwap.ai uses advanced AI technology to analyze your photo and virtually apply different clothing items. Our AI considers body shape, lighting, and pose to create realistic try-on experiences.
Is my photo data secure? +
Yes, we take privacy seriously. Your photos are processed securely and are not stored on our servers longer than necessary for processing. Please review our Privacy Policy for detailed information.
What devices are supported? +
LookSwap.ai is currently available for iOS devices. We support iPhone 8 and newer models running iOS 13.0 or later. Android support is coming soon!
How can I get better results? +
For best results, use well-lit photos with clear visibility of your body. Stand in a neutral pose facing the camera. Avoid busy backgrounds and ensure the photo is high quality.
Can I save and share my styled photos? +
Absolutely! You can save your styled photos to your device's photo library and share them directly to social media platforms or with friends through the app.
